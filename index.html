About WSP

Empowering the Next Generation of Entrepreneurs

World Student Pitch (WSP) is the premier platform for the brightest young minds to showcase their innovative business ideas. We connect student entrepreneurs with mentors, investors, and a global community to help turn their visions into reality.

Our mission is to foster the next generation of leaders and innovators by providing them with the resources and network necessary for success. Join us in building the future, one pitch at a time.
